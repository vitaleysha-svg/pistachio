## 2. THE STRATEGY (WHY THIS WORKS)

### THE MEAN FRAMEWORK
This is the core insight that makes this business work.

**Virality = Distance from the Average**

Content that looks like everything else gets ignored.
Content that deviates from the norm captures attention.

Example: @niabasic (AI influencer)
- Ultra-dark skin tone = far from the average AI model
- This deviation creates stopping power
- People pause their scroll

Most AI influencers fail because they create "generic beautiful woman" personas.
These look like stock photos. They blend in. No one cares.

Our persona will have distinctive, memorable features that create deviation:
- Specific ethnic mix (Egyptian/Brazilian)
- Physical quirks (tooth gap, frizzy hair)
- Personality positioning (accessible, not aspirational)
- Aesthetic (messy bedroom, not studio)

### THE "8/10 WHO THINKS SHE'S A 6/10" POSITIONING
Most AI influencers position as "perfect 10" unattainable fantasy.
This actually reduces engagement because it feels fake and distant.

Our positioning: The naturally beautiful girl who doesn't know she's beautiful.
- Doesn't wear makeup
- Doesn't try to look perfect
- Has a messy room
- Seems approachable
- Feels like someone you could actually meet

This creates parasocial connection. Followers feel like they "know" her.
That connection drives conversion to paid content.

### WHY AI INFLUENCERS WORK
1. No scheduling conflicts - content can be generated anytime
2. Perfect consistency - same face, same brand, always
3. Infinite scalability - one person can run multiple personas
4. Lower cost - no model fees, location costs, photographer fees
5. Full control - character never has bad days or scandals

### WHY MOST FAIL
1. Images look obviously AI (plastic skin, perfect symmetry)
2. No face consistency (different person in every image)
3. Generic positioning (looks like every other AI model)
4. No conversion funnel (just posts content, hopes for the best)
5. Wrong platform (OnlyFans is risky for AI content)

We're solving all five problems.

## 5. IMAGE GENERATION (MIDJOURNEY MASTERY)

This section contains everything needed to generate photorealistic images
that don't look like AI. - Depending on the tier of what I select, this will cost from anywhere between $8 to $50 a month. I will start with the $30 package as it gives us HD options, and go from there.

### THE CORE PROBLEM
AI images look fake because of:
1. Plastic/waxy skin - over-smoothed, no pores
2. Perfect symmetry - real faces are asymmetrical
3. No imperfections - missing wrinkles, freckles, blemishes
4. Flat lighting - unrealistic shadow placement
5. Oversaturation - colors too vibrant
6. High-frequency digital detail - looks 3D rendered

The solution: Prompt for imperfections FIRST, use specific settings,
and understand what makes images look photographic vs AI.

### THE GOLDEN RULE
**Imperfections = Real**
**Perfection = AI**

Lead with flaws in your prompts. The AI naturally smooths and perfects.
You must counter-prompt with specific imperfections.

### CRITICAL MIDJOURNEY SETTINGS

**Setting: --style raw**
What it does: Removes Midjourney's automatic "beautification"
Why essential: Without this, Midjourney adds artistic flair that screams AI
Always use: Yes, on every photorealistic prompt

**Setting: --s (stylize)**
Recommended value: 50-150
What it does: Controls how much artistic interpretation Midjourney adds
Lower = more realistic, follows prompt closely
Higher = more artistic, Midjourney takes liberties
For photorealism: Use 50 or 100

**Setting: --q (quality)**
Recommended value: 2
What it does: Controls rendering quality and detail
Higher = more texture detail, better lighting
Values: 0.25 (draft), 1 (standard), 2 (high quality)
For photorealism: Always use 2

**Setting: --ar (aspect ratio)**
Recommended value: 4:5 or 3:4
What it does: Sets image dimensions
4:5 is standard Instagram portrait ratio
3:4 is classic photo print ratio
For portraits: Use 4:5

**Setting: --no (negative prompt)**
What it does: Tells Midjourney what to avoid
Essential for removing AI artifacts
See negative prompts section below

### THE SETTINGS STACK (USE THIS ON EVERY PROMPT)
`--style raw --s 100 --q 2 --ar 4:5`

### PROMPT STRUCTURE
A photorealistic prompt has this structure:

[Subject with physical details and imperfections],
[Clothing and styling],
[Expression and pose],
[Camera and lens specification],
[Lighting description],
[Background/environment],
[Film stock or color treatment],
[Settings]
[Negative prompts]

### CAMERA TERMINOLOGY THAT WORKS
AI has been trained on millions of photos with EXIF data.
Naming specific cameras and lenses dramatically increases realism.

**Camera bodies to use:**
- Canon 5D Mark IV (warm, cinematic tones)
- Sony A7R IV (sharp detail, neutral)
- Fujifilm X-T5 (film-like colors)
- Hasselblad 500C/M (medium format, timeless)

**Lens specifications:**
- 85mm f/1.4 (classic portrait, creamy bokeh)
- 85mm f/1.8 (sharp portrait lens)
- 50mm f/1.4 (natural perspective)
- 35mm f/1.4 (environmental portrait)

**Aperture effects:**
- f/1.2 to f/2: Extreme background blur, dreamy
- f/2.8 to f/4: Moderate blur, more sharpness
- f/5.6+: Deep focus, not recommended for portraits

**Film stocks for color:**
- Kodak Portra 400 (warm skin tones, forgiving)
- Fujifilm Pro 400H (natural, soft pastels)
- Kodak Ektachrome E100 (vibrant, high contrast)

### SKIN TEXTURE KEYWORDS (CRITICAL)
Instead of generic descriptions, use specific texture language:

**DO USE:**
- visible skin pores
- fine lines
- subtle blemishes
- natural skin variation
- slight asymmetry
- realistic sheen
- sub-surface scattering skin
- age-appropriate texture

**DO NOT USE:**
- perfect skin
- flawless
- smooth
- airbrushed
- beautiful (triggers beautification)
- gorgeous (triggers beautification)

### IMPERFECTION KEYWORDS (CRITICAL)
Lead your prompts with these:

**Facial:**
- tooth gap diastema
- asymmetrical features
- one eyebrow slightly higher
- subtle under-eye circles
- natural expression lines
- visible pores

**Hair:**
- frizzy unstyled hair
- stray hairs
- hair flyaways
- natural hair texture
- not salon-perfect

**Expression:**
- tired expression
- bored expression
- candid moment
- not posed
- looking away from camera
- natural resting face

### LIGHTING KEYWORDS
Realistic lighting makes or breaks photorealism.

**Natural light:**
- soft window light from camera left
- golden hour light
- overcast daylight
- dappled sunlight through blinds

**Artificial light:**
- warm tungsten lamp lighting
- practical lighting (lamps visible in scene)
- dim ambient lighting
- single overhead light source

**Avoid:**
- ring light (screams "influencer fake")
- studio lighting (unless that's the context)
- flat lighting
- no shadows

### BACKGROUND KEYWORDS
Backgrounds signal "real" or "fake" immediately.

**Realistic backgrounds:**
- messy bedroom with authentic clutter
- unmade bed visible
- clothes on floor
- posters on walls
- practical lamps
- gaming setup
- natural window light

**Avoid:**
- plain white background
- studio backdrop
- perfectly clean room
- stock photo environments
- generic interior

### NEGATIVE PROMPT (USE ON EVERY IMAGE)
This removes AI artifacts. Copy exactly:

`--no plastic, oversaturated, airbrushed, waxy, over-smoothed, flat lighting, artificial, 3D rendered, glossy, shiny, uncanny, filters, photoshop, makeup, perfect, beautiful, symmetrical, smooth skin, studio lighting, ring light`

## 6. FACE CONSISTENCY (COMFYUI + INSTANTID)

### THE PROBLEM
Midjourney generates different faces every time.
Even with the same prompt and seed, faces vary.
This breaks the illusion of a real person.

### THE SOLUTION
Use ComfyUI with InstantID and IP-Adapter to lock in a consistent face.
Once you have the "hero image" from Midjourney, use that face for all future images.

### TECHNIQUE COMPARISON

| Method | Difficulty | Quality | Speed | Training Required | Best For |
|--------|------------|---------|-------|-------------------|----------|
| InstantID | Easy (5-10 min setup) | 82-86% consistency | 12-18 sec/image | None | Fast iterations, angle variations |
| IP-Adapter FaceID Plus V2 | Easy (5-10 min setup) | 85-92% consistency | 15-30 sec/image | None | Best balance of speed and quality |
| LoRA Training | Medium (3-5 hours total) | 95-98% consistency | Normal speed after training | 3-5 hours with 15-30 images | Maximum consistency for long-term character |

### RECOMMENDATION
Start with InstantID + IP-Adapter (no training, good results).
If consistency isn't good enough, invest in LoRA training later.

   - Blinking

   - Add music/sound
   - Slight grain filter
   - Match color grade to stills
