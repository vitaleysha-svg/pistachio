## Week 1 Predictions

### P1: First images will look too AI
**Prediction:** Initial Midjourney outputs will still have "AI shine" despite DSLR skeleton prompt
**Confidence:** 8/10
**Timeframe:** Day 1-3
**What to watch:** Skin texture, symmetry, background realism
**If true:** Iterate on prompt, add more imperfection terms, try --style raw with lower --s

### P2: Face consistency will be harder than expected
**Prediction:** Even with InstantID, getting 100% consistent faces across poses will require multiple iterations
**Confidence:** 7/10
**Timeframe:** Week 1
**What to watch:** Face shape drift, eye color changes, skin tone variation
**If true:** Invest in LoRA training sooner rather than later

### P3: Wan2.1 video quality won't match stills
**Prediction:** Video generation will be noticeably lower quality than still images
**Confidence:** 8/10
**Timeframe:** First video test
**What to watch:** Face distortion during movement, background artifacts
**If true:** Use very subtle motion only (breathing, blinking). Consider Runway as backup.
