## Channel #3: AI Avatars / UGC Creators for Small Businesses (B2B)

**Date Added:** 2026-02-07
**Status:** IDEA - Validated by user as intentional direction

### The Concept
Use Pistachio's AI pipeline to create AI influencers, avatars, and UGC-style content for small businesses (dealerships, local businesses, e-commerce brands). These businesses can't afford real influencers or UGC creators - we generate them.

### Why This Works
- Small businesses need video content but can't produce it (the Space Auto problem - owner has to shoot everything himself)
- AI-generated UGC creators = unlimited content at near-zero cost
- We're already building the pipeline for Pistachio's own personas
- Same tech, different client: instead of monetizing the persona on Fanvue, we deploy it for a business's social media/ads

### Revenue Model (To Be Defined)
- Monthly retainer: Create X pieces of AI UGC content per month for a business
- Per-asset pricing: Charge per video/image generated
- AI avatar creation: One-time fee to create a custom AI persona for a brand + monthly content generation
- Could bundle with RedLine Gen's ad management for dealerships (Pistachio produces the creative, RedLine Gen runs the ads) - but they remain SEPARATE businesses

### Profitability Gates
- [ ] Pistachio pipeline produces photorealistic UGC-quality content
- [ ] Can generate varied scenarios (different settings, outfits, poses) reliably
- [ ] At least one successful deployment (test with Space Auto or another business)
- [ ] Pricing validated against market (what do real UGC creators charge? $150-500/video)

### Connection to Other Businesses
- Pistachio creates the AI avatars/content (the tech)
- RedLine Gen could be a distribution channel (offer AI creative to ad management clients)
- They are SEPARATE businesses but Pistachio's output can serve RedLine Gen's clients
- This is NOT a merge - it's Pistachio selling a product that RedLine Gen's clients happen to need

## Channel #4: Course / Education Product

**Date Added:** 2026-02-07
**Status:** BUILDING CONTENT NOW (passively - everything we document = course material)

### The Concept
Sell a course teaching people how to build AI influencer businesses. The Pistachio knowledge base IS the course - simplified and packaged for buyers who don't want to figure it all out themselves.

### Why This Works
- We're documenting EVERYTHING as we build (pipeline workflow, revenue projections, tool comparisons, scripts, strategies)
- Every problem we solve = a lesson in the course
- "We did it, here's exactly how, here's the revenue proof" = the ultimate sales pitch
- The course simplifies what took us months into a step-by-step system
- Market is hungry: AI influencer tutorials on YouTube get millions of views
- Buyers pay to SKIP the learning curve we're going through right now

### What the Course Would Cover
- Module 1: Face creation (Midjourney hero image)
- Module 2: ComfyUI pipeline setup (InstantID, settings, workflow)
- Module 3: Image generation at scale (scenarios, batching, quality control)
- Module 4: Video generation (WAN 2.2, Kling, HeyGen)
- Module 5: Voice and talking head videos
- Module 6: Content strategy (posting schedule, Instagram/TikTok growth)
- Module 7: Monetization (Fanvue setup, pricing, PPV, tips)
- Module 8: Automation (scaling to 99% automated)
- Module 9: Multi-channel expansion (B2B service, UGC for businesses)

### Revenue Model
- Self-paced course: $297-497 one-time
- Premium with community/support: $997
- Monthly membership with updates: $47-97/mo
- Even 50 sales at $297 = $14,850

### Profitability Gates
- [ ] Pistachio Channel 1 generating proven revenue (need the case study)
- [ ] Pipeline documented end-to-end (we're doing this NOW)
- [ ] At least 3 months of revenue data to show as proof
- [ ] Simplified workflow tested (can a beginner follow it?)

### Key Insight
We're NOT waiting to build the course. We're building it RIGHT NOW by documenting everything in the knowledge base. When the time comes, we package what already exists. Zero additional content creation needed - just format and simplify.

## Channel #6: Website Funnel → Conversion Engine

**Date Added:** 2026-02-07
**Status:** IDEA - From Matt
**Source:** Matt (business partner)

### The Concept
Build a website that funnels visitors into an end goal (subscription, service purchase, content access). The website acts as a conversion engine - driving traffic from Instagram/TikTok/ads to a landing page that moves them to Fanvue, service inquiries, or course purchases.

### Why This Works
- Social media profiles have limited link/CTA options
- A website gives full control over the funnel (A/B testing, pixel tracking, retargeting)
- Can serve multiple channels: Fanvue sub, B2B service inquiry, course purchase
- Adds legitimacy and professionalism to any outreach
- Enables email capture (build owned audience, not rented)

### Potential Funnel Paths
- Instagram/TikTok → Website → Fanvue subscription
- Ads → Landing page → B2B service inquiry (Channel 2/3)
- Organic search → Course sales page → Purchase (Channel 4)
- Website → Email list → Nurture sequence → Multiple conversion paths

### Profitability Gates
- [ ] At least one channel generating revenue
- [ ] Clear conversion goal defined (what are we funneling TO?)
- [ ] Traffic source identified (organic vs paid)
- [ ] Landing page built and tested

### Tools
- GoHighLevel (already paying for it) can build landing pages/funnels
- Or standalone: Carrd ($19/yr), Framer, Webflow
- Email: Already have Twilio SendGrid
