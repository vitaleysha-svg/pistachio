# Hooks & Content Templates Library

> Master reference for video ad scripts and content creation.
> Use these templates adapted for automotive/dealership context.

## TikTok-Style Video Hooks (Proven Viral Templates)

### Problem-Solution Hooks
- "If you feel like [problem], I'm changing your life in 1 video"
- "Here's how to [benefit] even if you're [what avatar believes]"
- "If you're trying to [result] but it hasn't worked - I'm here to save you"
- "The reason you don't have [result] is because of [thing]. Here's how to fix it"
- "You [describe undesired situation]. I got you."
- "You [describe goal]. I got you."

### Controversy/Pattern-Interrupt Hooks
- "This is kinda controversial but it saved me [thing]"
- "You've been lied to about X and it's about time someone told you the truth"
- "I've never shared this publicly but one of the biggest lies I fell for was [lie]"
- "Chances are you're not [thing they think] you're actually [new thing]"

### Gatekeeping/Secret Hooks
- "I'm done gatekeeping. Here's how to [specific promise]"
- "I'm done gatekeeping. Here's how to [promise] without [things people hate]"
- "My friends convinced me to spill the beans. Here's how to [result]"
- "Here's a weird, little known secret about X you've probably never heard before"
- "The #1 secret to [result]"

### Authority/Results Hooks
- "Here's a [state what they get]"
- "Top 3 ways to [result] without [thing people hate]"
- "Here's a quick way to [result]"
- "Do [result] like [person with result]"
- "Here's how to get rid of [bad thing] once and for all"
- "This is the single easiest way to [result]"

### Targeting/Callout Hooks
- "This video is for you if you [situation]"
- "This video is for you if you [problem]"
- "If you're still complaining about [problem] as [avatar]. It's time to grow up"
- "You're trying way too hard to [result]"
- "You can [result] even if you don't [have skill]"

### Mistake/Myth Hooks
- "This is the biggest mistake you can make when it comes to X"
- "Here are the 3 most common mistakes [avatar] makes when it comes to [result]"
- "I don't know why more people aren't talking about [specific thing]"
- "So a lot of people understand [common belief]. But here's the #1 sign that [new term]"

### Empowerment Hooks
- "How even [avatar] can get [result]. Yes it's possible"
- "I just met a guy that [benefit avatar wants] through [weird thing]"
- "Why you need to do [seemingly unrelated thing] if you want to [result]"
- "Here's how to [do something against all rules] and get [result]"

## 81 Viral Content Templates

### How-To / Step-by-Step
- "How to [result]"
- "[#] Steps to [result]"
- "How I went from X to X"
- "If I had to [result] from scratch, here's what I'd do"
- "If I had to [result] in [time constraint], here's what I'd do"

### Before/After / Transformation
- "I was/now. Here's how I did it"
- "I used to/now. Here's how I did it"
- "Achievement. Here's what happened"
- "Achievement. Observations"

### List / Ranking
- "Top 10/3 [things]"
- "10/3 Best tools for [result]"
- "10/3 Lessons from [experience]"
- "10/7/3 Hacks that [result]"
- "10/3 Cheat codes for [result]"
- "Here are 10/3 X ideas nobody talks about anymore"

### Myth-Busting / Contrarian
- "People think [common opinion]. Here's why that's not the case"
- "Myth blaster: [belief] is a myth. Here's why"
- "Why [common belief] is a waste of time"
- "Why [major problem] is totally unnecessary"
- "3 beliefs hurting [result] and 3 to replace them with"

### Testimonial / Social Proof
- "Testimonial (you focused): How I [result]"
- "Testimonial (client focused): How [name] [result]"
- "I asked [#] people [question]. Here's what they told me"

### Nostalgia / Trend
- "Who remembers [nostalgic thing]. Here's why I loved it"
- "Trend. Here's what it could mean for you"
- "Trend. Here's how to take advantage of it"

### Authority / Mentor
- "Here's what my [result] mentor told me about [topic]"
- "A [result] mentor taught me the secrets of [topic]"
- "Why X is the most underrated X"
- "I've tried X different X. Here's what's worked best"

### Common Mistakes
- "Common mistakes in [area]"
- "3 things you're doing that's stopping you from [result]"
- "Here is the ONLY tactic you need for [result]"

### Income/Revenue Revealing
- "Anything income revealing: How much I made [doing thing]"
