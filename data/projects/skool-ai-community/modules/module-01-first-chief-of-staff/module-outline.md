# Module 01 - Your First AI Chief of Staff

## Outcome
By the end of this module, the member has:
1. Their first personalized AI action plan.
2. A working baseline CLAUDE.md instruction.
3. A repeatable daily check-in flow.

## Duration
30-40 minutes total.

## Lessons
1. `lesson-01-mental-model.md` (5 min)
2. `welcome-video-script.md` (90 sec script)
3. `five-minute-win-walkthrough.md` (hands-on, 10 min)
4. `claude-md-template.md` (copy/paste setup)
5. `daily-log-template.md` (first daily operating file)
6. `installation-guide.md` (browser-first path + optional CLI upgrade)
7. `lesson-06-first-week-routine.md` (10 min)
8. `module-checklist.md`

## Assignment
- Complete the 5-minute win.
- Post your result in `#wins` using this format:
  - My role:
  - My ONE THING this week:
  - My 3-step plan:
  - My biggest surprise:

## Pass Criteria
- Member can show the generated plan.
- Member created a daily log entry.
- Member can run the same prompt tomorrow with updated context.
