# Exercise - Weekly Planning with Chief of Staff

## Input prompt
```text
Here are my goals and patterns.
Build a weekly plan with:
1) My ONE THING
2) 3 supporting tasks
3) 3 non-goals
4) one daily checkpoint question I should answer every night
```

## Output quality check
- Is the ONE THING measurable?
- Are non-goals explicit?
- Are daily actions realistic for my schedule?
