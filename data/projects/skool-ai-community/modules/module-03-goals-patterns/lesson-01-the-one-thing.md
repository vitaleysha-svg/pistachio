# Lesson 01 - The ONE THING

People don't fail from lack of effort.
They fail from divided effort.

The ONE THING is the highest-value outcome that makes other things easier.

## Prompt
```text
Given my goals below, identify the ONE THING for this week.
Then list 3 tasks I should explicitly ignore.
```

If AI cannot tell you what to ignore, your system is still too vague.
