# Exercise - Your First Five Mistakes

## Objective
Build your first compounding quality loop.

## Steps
1. Open your last 7 AI conversations.
2. Pick the 5 most expensive mistakes (time, stress, wrong output).
3. Write one rule per mistake using the template.
4. Re-run one task with new rules active.
5. Compare old output vs new output.

## Reflection Prompt
What mistake keeps repeating because you never turned it into a rule?

## Done When
- 5 mistakes are logged.
- You can point to 1 output that got materially better.
