# Community Guidelines

## What this community is
A practical implementation community.
We ship systems, not theory.

## Rules
1. Be specific. Share what you ran and what happened.
2. Post wins and blockers with context.
3. Respect member privacy and unpublished work.
4. No spam, no self-promo without permission.
5. Keep feedback direct and useful.

## Posting Format
- Goal:
- What I ran:
- Result:
- Blocker:
- Next action:

## Fastest way to get help
Share your exact prompt, your output, and the expected outcome.
