# Community Operations SOP (First 100 Members)

## Daily Cadence
1. Morning post: priority prompt or challenge.
2. Midday check: answer unresolved questions.
3. Evening close: highlight one member win.

## Response Standard
- Target first response within 4 hours.
- If blocked, acknowledge and give exact follow-up time.

## Weekly Cadence
- One live implementation call.
- One win spotlight post.
- One challenge tied to module completion.
- One feedback thread.

## Moderator Rules
- Keep discussions practical.
- Remove fluff and off-topic spam.
- Escalate technical blockers to implementation threads.

## Escalation Ladder
1. Member confusion -> direct them to specific lesson + timestamp.
2. Repeated blocker -> short Loom or text walkthrough.
3. Systemic blocker -> update template/module and notify all members.
