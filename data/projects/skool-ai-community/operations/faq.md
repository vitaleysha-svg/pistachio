# FAQ

## Do I need technical skills?
No. You can start browser-first.

## How long until I see value?
First win is designed for under 5 minutes.

No. It's a full operating system with workflows and templates.

Minimum: 60-90 minutes for core loops.

## What if I miss a week?
Use the weekly review template and restart from Module 1 checkpoint.
