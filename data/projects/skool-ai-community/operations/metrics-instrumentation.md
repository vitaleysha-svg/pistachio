# Metrics Instrumentation Plan

## Core KPIs
1. Activation rate: % members who finish 5-minute win in first 48h.
2. Module 1 completion rate.
3. Module 1-3 completion rate.
4. Day 7 active rate.
5. Month 1 churn.

## KPI Targets
- Activation >= 70%
- Module 1 completion >= 50%
- Module 1-3 completion >= 40% by month 1
- Day 7 active >= 30%
- Month 1 churn <= 12%

## Event Log (minimum)
- member_joined
- first_win_posted
- module_completed
- live_call_attended
- churned

## Weekly Review Questions
1. Where are people dropping?
2. Which lesson creates the highest win rate?
3. Which step creates the most support load?
4. What one fix gives the biggest retention gain?
