# Onboarding Automation Blueprint

## Trigger 1 - Member joins
Action:
- Send welcome message with Module 1 link.
- Ask for ONE THING in one sentence.

## Trigger 2 - 24 hours no activity
Action:
- Send Day 1 nudge: "Run the 5-minute win now."

## Trigger 3 - Completed first post
Action:
- Award quick-start badge.
- Point them to Module 2.

## Trigger 4 - Day 7 reached
Action:
- Prompt weekly review template.
- Ask for one win + one friction point.

## Required Tracking Fields
- join_date
- first_win_date
- module_1_complete
- module_3_complete
- day_7_active
