# Risk and Rollback Playbook

## Top Risks
1. Members feel overwhelmed in week 1.
2. Completion rates drop below target.
3. Support load exceeds response capacity.
4. Content sounds generic and trust drops.

## Early Warning Signals
- Activation < 50%
- Same question repeated by 5+ members
- Day 7 active < 20%
- Multiple refund requests with same complaint

## Rollback Actions
1. Reduce onboarding steps to core 3 (win, log, brief).
2. Pause advanced modules from default path.
3. Replace long lessons with short implementation checklists.
4. Add one live "setup clinic" session.
