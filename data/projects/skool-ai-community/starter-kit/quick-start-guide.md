# Quick Start Guide (Do These 5 Steps In Order)

## Step 1 - Set your ONE THING (5 min)
Fill `goals-template.md`.

## Step 2 - Paste the CLAUDE.md setup (5 min)
Use `claude-md-non-technical.md` as your baseline system instruction.

## Step 3 - Run your first morning brief (5 min)
Use `morning-brief-template.md`.

## Step 4 - Start your daily log (5 min)
Create today's entry with `daily-log-template.md`.

## Step 5 - Add your first learned mistake (5 min)
Use `learned-mistakes-template.md`.

## Done Definition
You are live when:
- ONE THING is defined.
- Morning brief exists.
- Daily log exists.
- First learned mistake is logged.

That's it.
