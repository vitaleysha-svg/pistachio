## Pre-Lander Strategy

### What Makes a Good Pre-Lander

1. **Teaser content** - Blur or preview of what's inside
2. **Social proof** - Subscriber count, testimonials
3. **Scarcity/urgency** - Limited spots, special offer
4. **Clear CTA** - One button, one action
5. **Mobile-optimized** - 80%+ traffic is mobile
